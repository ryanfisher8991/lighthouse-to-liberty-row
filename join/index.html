Eligibility Requirements
  • Both rowers must be members of the South Jersey Chiefs Association or United States Lifeguard Association
  • Boat must be a qualified Van Dyne or AC type lifeguard dory
  • At least one rower in the boat must have completed a 15+ mile row in a single sitting with two rowers
  • Rowers must trailer their own boat to the meeting location and have a trailer driver to move it to the pickup location
Race Day Instructions

Launch time: Before sunrise on September 5, 2026. Exact time will be communicated after registration.

Meeting location: Sandy Hook Boat Ramp. You are responsible for trailering your boat there. Your trailer driver must be available to move the trailer to the Port Liberty Boat Ramp pickup point.

Finish: Liberty Island. After the finish we will exit at Port Liberty Boat Ramp (1.5 miles).

Safety: All participants must have appropriate safety gear. This is an open ocean row — conditions may be rough. Participation is at your own risk.
